# Style DNA

## One Sentence

Pure white, minimalist, hand-drawn, white space, restrained, absurd, product-sketch feel, handwritten annotation feel, structurally clear but not instructional.

Like someone who works in AI, product, design, or dev tools casually sketching an explanation on white paper.

## Must Have

- 16:9 horizontal editorial illustration.
- Pure white background: no cream, warm gray, paper texture, gradient, shadow, noise, or vintage paper feel.
- Black hand-drawn line art as primary: thin lines, slight wobble, not mechanical, not vector, not thick outlines.
- Lots of white space: main subject occupies about 40%-60% of the canvas, at least 35% blank white, ideally one large quiet area.
- Sparse handwritten English annotations: maximum 5-8 labels, each ideally 2-6 words.
- One image explains only one core action, structure, state, or metaphor.
- Structure should be expressed naturally — don't write the structure type name on the image.

## Colors

- Black: main line art, characters, frames, structures, primary text, main objects.
- Red: key annotations, problems, emotional points, critical reminders, results.
- Orange: main flow/path, arrows, automation direction, A-to-B movement relationships.
- Blue: supplementary notes, mental states, system states, second-layer explanations, AI/assistant/automation hints.

Blue is not required in every image. Colors should be restrained — less is more.

## Absolute Don'ts

- No commercial illustration.
- No PPT infographics.
- No formal flowcharts.
- No course slides or courseware.
- No cute cartoon posters.
- No children's illustration.
- No complex architecture diagrams.
- No polished flat illustration.
- No tech-feel UI.
- No realistic app screenshots.
- No complex backgrounds, gradients, shadows, or textures.
- No explaining every node in detail.
- No writing type-label titles in the top-left corner like "Workflow / System Architecture / Common Pitfalls / Roadmap".

## Aesthetic Direction

Aim for: absurd, inventive, interesting, clean, lots of breathing room, wildly imaginative.

Avoid: cute, childish, overly complex, stiff.
